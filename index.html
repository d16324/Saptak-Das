üî¨ Research Activity

1. Bayesian Optimization‚Äìbased Neural-Symbolic Artificial Intelligence (BONSAI) for Explainable Learning from Urban Data Guide: Dr. Monidipa Das, IISER Kolkata, India

Goal: This research focuses on developing a Bayesian optimization‚Äìdriven neural-symbolic AI framework to enable explainable, efficient learning from complex urban datasets. The objective is to integrate probabilistic optimization with symbolic reasoning to enhance model transparency, interpretability, and robustness, thereby supporting informed and transparent urban planning and decision-making processes.

2. Clustering and Analysis of High-Dimensional Gene Score Data for Target Cell Classification
Guides: Dr. P. Majumder, Dr. N. Sadananda Shing, IISER Thiruvananthapuram

Goal: This project explored clustering methodologies for high-dimensional biological data (19,814 √ó 769 gene score matrix) to identify meaningful subgroups within complex gene expression profiles. By grouping structurally similar data points, the work aimed to uncover latent biological patterns that could provide insights into cellular classification and biological processes.

3. A study to predict the impact of various environmental parameters on daily COVID-19 positive cases with a performance comparison of various machine learning tools Guide: Dr. P. Majumder, IISER Thiruvananthapuram

Goal: The thesis investigated the role of meteorological and environmental variables in influencing COVID-19 transmission dynamics in Kolkata. Multiple machine learning models were evaluated to identify optimal predictive frameworks, with the goal of informing data-driven public health interventions and future decision-making strategies.
